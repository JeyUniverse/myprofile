## Step-by-Step Process for Logging In and Booking a Reservation

1. Login

* Open the web application.

* Enter your email and password in the login form.

* Click the "Login" button.

2. Access the Booking Page

* After logging in, go to the "Book Reservation" section from the dashboard menu.

3. Fill Out the Reservation Form

* Enter your name, contact details, and purpose.

* Choose your desired date and time.

* Select a driver from the available options.

* Select a vehicle from the list.

4. Submit the Reservation

* Review all the information.

* Click the "Submit Reservation" button.

5. Confirmation

* A message will confirm that your reservation is pending approval.

* You can view the status in the "Reservation Status" section.
